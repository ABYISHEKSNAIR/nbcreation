    ABOUT US

    NB creation designs garments to provide the Comfort, durability and design required by integrating manufacturing, production, finishing and customer Service. Excellent quality products are provided at Economical rates by having end to end solutions from yarn to finished garments under one roof. By optimizing technology, manpower and Management, NB creation ensures fine garments That meet the needs of today’s requirement effectively. NB creation has been providing clothing solutions to meet the requirements of both domestic and international markets. NB creation specializes in Corporate Wear, Uniforms for industries, hotels, hospitals and educational institutions. We have possessed years of experience in this industry, which has enabled us to understand the latest business dynamics and stay ahead of our competitors

    MANUFACTURING UNIT

    NB creation is abided by great experience & expertise in manufacturing unique Uniforms for different streams. The uniforms offered by us are widely applauded in the market for their features like shrink resistance, high tear strength, neat stitching, well fitted, comfortable to wear, skin friendly, perfect fit, light in weight, softness and colour fastness. It is dedicated to manufacturing the finest garments at economical prices and providing high quality service, management and timely delivery. Core technologies and manpower is effectively integrated with responsive and responsible management. Each stage of the production is optimized for Quality and leads to making the finished Product a fine garment. The yarn is tested at the manufacturing stage itself and is tested for proper count, colour fastness and strength. Design combinations can be integrated into the manufacturing process to ensure consistency in the fabric and finished garments under the supervision of diligent craftsmen who are well versed in this domain. Embroidery, decorations and other value addition are available. Special and custom garments are also manufactured depending on the sizes, complexity and quantity needed
